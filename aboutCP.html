Planning Your Visit

Travel

The University of Maryland and The Hotel are located less than one mile from the College Park Metro station, which is located on Metro's Green Line. Campus is also located near the three major airports that serve the Baltimore-Washington, DC area. Complimentary shuttle service is available for The Hotel guests, and it offers transportation to the College Park Metro Station and access to other destinations within a three-mile radius.

  • Ronald Reagan National Airport (DCA); 17 miles
  • Baltimore/Washington International Airport (BWI); 27 miles
  • Washington Dulles International Airport (IAD); 36 miles

Parking

Daily commuters are encouraged to park at The Hotel at the University of Maryland. The daily parking for attendees at The Hotel as been reduced to $10 per day. Commuters may collect a validation sticker for their parking ticket at the conference registration/information table daily in order to receive the reduced rate. Campus parking is available in Lot 1 without a permit during the conference dates. Commuters may also park in the visitor parking area located on the top level of the Regents Drive Garage. Visitor parking lot fees are $3 per hour (no daily maximum). Access campus parking maps.
