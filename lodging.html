The official conference hotel is The Hotel at the University of Maryland which is new and just across Baltimore Avenue from campus. A limited number of rooms are available for conference attendees.

Reserve a Room


When reserving a room via the reservation link, please enter your reservation details (i.e., number of nights), review your check in and check out dates on the next screen, and then click on "Show Rooms" to select your room preference.
