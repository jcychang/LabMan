Lodging

LabMan 2018 attendees are strongly encouraged to stay at the official conference hotel, The Hotel at the University of Maryland.

7777 Baltimore Avenue
College Park, Maryland 20740
301-277-7777


The official conference hotel is The Hotel at the University of Maryland which is new and just across Baltimore Avenue from campus. A limited number of rooms are available for conference attendees.

Rates: $179/night (plus applicable taxes)

Reserve a Room


When reserving a room via the reservation link, please enter your reservation details (i.e., number of nights), review your check in, check out dates on the next screen, and then click on "Show Rooms" to select your room preference.

You may also call 844.954.6835 to make a reservation using booking code 115573.
