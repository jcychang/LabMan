Conference Registration Fees

Your registration fee includes access to all sessions and conference events, including the visit to historic Annapolis and dinner at Buddy's Crabs & Ribs. Some meals will be on your own.

Early (on or before April 24) - $225

Standard (April 24 - May 15) - $275

Presenter Registration is $50 off (a discount code will be provided to presenters)

Additional Fees

You may bring a guest with you to the Annapolis trip and dinner for an additional $65 (this includes round trip transportation), which can be paid during registration. This option will not be available to purchase onsite.
